02 Our story

A standard that inspires — and lasts.

In the commercial world, to confidently explore your market, you need a partner who combines in-depth design and corporate identity knowledge with a clear brand-building perspective.

We established in 1992, formerly known as Circus Design. Our experienced client servicing and creative teams have been supporting business in design and production for over 20 years. We support all your marketing needs — corporate ID & branding, display units and showcases, advertising and collateral design, product packaging, multimedia, event production, exhibition, shop navigation, and photography.

Our works of corporate branding can inspire your company in the same way a standard can inspire a country. Circus One adds values to all business.

03 The journey

Three decades, one studio.

A short history of how Circus One became what it is today.

1992

Founded as Circus Design in Hong Kong.

2000s

Expanded from branding into packaging, 3D display, and events for the spirits sector.

2010s

Added web, multimedia, and digital to the in-house offer. Rebranded as Circus One Limited.

Today

A multi-disciplinary studio trusted by global spirits houses, property groups, and local businesses alike.

04 What we believe

Four principles, kept since 1992.

They're the reason our clients stay — and the reason we keep winning new ones.

01

Relationships first

We invest in long-term partnerships, not project-by-project transactions. Most of our clients have been with us for years.

02

Craft, always

Every deliverable — from a luxury pack to a local shop sign — gets the same attention to detail. Standards don't slide.

03

Strategy before pixels

We start with the business problem. Aesthetics follow from understanding, not the other way around.

04

In-house, end-to-end

Seven disciplines under one roof. No briefs lost between agencies, no inconsistent voices across your brand.
