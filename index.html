04 Our story

From Circus Design in 1992 to Circus One today.

We started as a small Hong Kong studio with a simple belief: design should add value, not decoration. Three decades on, that belief still drives every project — whether we're crafting a luxury spirit's heritage pack or a local shop's window display.

Our client servicing and creative teams have supported businesses across spirits, property, hospitality, FMCG, and government for over 20 years. We build relationships first; the work follows.
